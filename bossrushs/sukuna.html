Sukono, King of Curses

Taking place on Shibuya Aftermath, the player must defend against 30 waves of enemies, with Sukono appearing on each 10th Wave. Every time Sokono appears, he initiates multiple of his Attacks ranging from Cleave and Dismantle Slam to Cleaving Dash and Flame Release.

Each time Sukono is defeated, the player is given the option to Disable one of these attacks. Cleave, Dismantle Slam and Cleaving Dash are very similar in their effects, while Flame Release makes the player unable to sell their units, so this option is recommended to disable first.
