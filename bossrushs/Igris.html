Igros, The Blood-Red Commander

Taking place within the Igros Castle, the player must defend against 30 waves of enemies, with Igros appearing on each 10th Wave. Every time Igros appears, he becomes stronger, and with each Bar that is taken from his health, he stuns all nearby units. Defeating Igros 3 times completes the stage as a victory.
