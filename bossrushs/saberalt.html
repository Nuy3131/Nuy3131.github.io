Dark-Tainted Tyrant, Saber (Alternate)

Taking place in the Cavern, the player must defend against 30 waves of enemies, with Saber (Alternate) appearing on each 10th Wave. On wave 10, she has her default health value. Upon reaching wave 15, the first A.W.E mini boss spawns at the enemy spawn portal. Defeating this A.W.E reduces Saber's (Alternate) health when she spawns in on wave 20, this process also happens with wave 30. When Saber (Alternate) is alive, she occasionally does a Stun attack, similar to Igros.

Also included are Servants, allowing the player to choose a companion that acts as an ability they may activate or have active if they require assistance throughout the stage. An example of this is Berserker, allowing the player to deal damage in an AOE radius in any location they choose 3 times throughout the stage.
